BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German luxury automobile manufacturer founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. Today, BMW is one of the world's most respected automobile companies, famous for producing luxury cars, sports cars, SUVs, electric vehicles, and motorcycles. The company is known for combining performance, comfort, safety, advanced technology, and elegant design in every vehicle. BMW's famous slogan is "The Ultimate Driving Machine." This reflects the company's goal of providing an enjoyable driving experience through powerful engines, precise steering, advanced suspension systems, and innovative technologies. BMW operates in more than 140 countries and manufactures millions of vehicles every year. In addition to BMW cars, the company also owns MINI and Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, expanding its range from compact cars to ultra-luxury vehicles.
